Do you want to get all about the AKB48? Read tons of blog articles for member of AKB48 by using this application.

Configure your 'oshimems' to read articles for your prefered members and others separately. You can also choose a color for each member to indicates whose article.

Enabling push notification, you can receive oshimem's updates.

*MEMBER NAME AND BLOG ARTICLE IS DISPLAYED IN JAPANESE.
*PUSH NOTIFICATION FUNCTIONALITY IS BETA VERSION. IT MAY NOT WORK CORRECTLY.
